# ⚡ Quick Start - Email Setup

## 🎯 What You Need to Do

### 1️⃣ Set Up Cloudflare Email Routing (10 minutes)

**Go to:** https://dash.cloudflare.com  
**Navigate to:** Email → Email Routing  
**Click:** "Add records and enable"

**Create these addresses:**
- `info@tjenervikar.dk` → Forward to your Gmail
- `jobs@tjenervikar.dk` → Forward to your Gmail

**Verify:** Check Gmail for verification email and click the link

---

### 2️⃣ Set Up Web3Forms (5 minutes)

**Go to:** https://web3forms.com  
**Sign up** with your email

**Create 2 forms:**

**Form 1:** Contact Form
- Name: `Tjenervikar Contact`
- Email: `info@tjenervikar.dk`
- **Copy the Access Key** → Save it

**Form 2:** Job Applications
- Name: `Tjenervikar Jobs`
- Email: `jobs@tjenervikar.dk`
- **Copy the Access Key** → Save it

---

### 3️⃣ Update Website (2 minutes)

**Send your developer these 2 access keys:**
1. Contact Form Access Key: `[paste here]`
2. Job Application Access Key: `[paste here]`

They need to replace `YOUR_WEB3FORMS_ACCESS_KEY` in the HTML file.

---

### 4️⃣ Test Everything (2 minutes)

1. Go to https://tjenervikar.dk
2. Fill out the contact form → Check Gmail
3. Fill out job application form → Check Gmail
4. Done! ✅
